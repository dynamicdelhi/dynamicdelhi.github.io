OUR PRODUCTS


Electro Magnetic Bowl Feeders

Provide desired orientation of components in line feeding to processing assembly and packing operations at high feed rates.

Electro Magnetic Vibrators

Useful to provide a most effective method of obtaining the continuous and free flow of bulk materials from bins and hoppers, and through chutes.

Electro Magnetic Vibratory Table

Electromagnetic Vibratory Tables are mostly used in electrical & electronic assemblies where vibration testing is required to check dry soldering and is also useful in packing compact of fluffy and difficult to handle powders in industries.

Electro Magnetic Vibratory Feeders

Magnetic Coolant Filters

Designed for Filtering coolant oil contaminated with ferrous materials.

Drum Type Paper Filter

Designed to filter contaminated coolant/cutting oil with high degree of purification using paper as filter media to enhance life of coolant and improve the surface finish of the product.

Paper Band Filters

Designed for filtering ferrous and non-ferrous particles with high degree of purification.

Rare Earth Magnetic Coolant filters

Latest devlopment of with the use of super power rare earth magnets rhat is almost 8 times higher in force than that of conventional ferrite magnets.

Double Drum Magnetic Separator

Used for application requiring automatic seperation of 'several difficult to separate' magnetic particles from non magnetic materials, processesd in bulk quantity.

Permanent Magnetic Plates

Used for removing tramp iron such as Bolts, Nuts, Wires, Nails and other ferrous contaminations from the free flowing materials to protect machines.

Hopper Magnets

Used to remove iron particles from plastic, Rice, Dall, Flour etc. and Protect Extruders, Moulding Machines Rice/Flour/Dall Mills from Nails, Bolts, Nuts etc.

Magnetic Floor Sweeper

Used in collection of scraps from the shop floor, foundries & machine shops.

Magnetic Sheet Floats

Magnetic sheet floats are widely used in press shops. When a magnetic sheet float is placed along a stack of MS Sheet, it generates a gap between few upper most sheets and makes it easy to remove the upper sheet from the stack to feed the power press and in process.
